Capstone Case Study

Club Management Application

Project Statement

A hospitality operations platform designed to make communication, scheduling, and inventory awareness faster and more visible in high-pressure environments.

RolePlanning / Documentation / Dashboard Work
TypeTeam 37 Capstone
FocusOperations / Messaging / Scheduling
FormatMobile-first case study

Problem

Busy service environments break down when communication is fragmented.

Staff often rely on manual coordination, inconsistent updates, and limited visibility into inventory, assignments, and urgent operational issues.

Response

A role-based digital system for coordination, awareness, and response.

The project centers on messaging, role-based access, scheduling visibility, inventory management, and emergency alerts to support smoother operations.
